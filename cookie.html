Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Learn how EarnZone Pro uses cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience. EarnZone Pro uses cookies and similar technologies (such as localStorage) to keep you logged in and remember your settings.

Types of Cookies We Use

Third-Party Cookies

Our platform uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database services
  • Google Fonts: Font delivery (may set analytics cookies)
  • Adsterra / CPA Networks: Ad serving and offer tracking
  • ipapi.co: Country detection for payout rates

These third-party cookies are governed by their respective privacy policies, over which EarnZone Pro has no control.

Why We Use Cookies

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in between visits
  • Preferences: To remember your language and settings
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent account creation
  • Functionality: To enable referral tracking and offer completions

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may prevent EarnZone Pro from functioning correctly — in particular, authentication cookies are required to stay logged in.

To clear cookies, go to your browser's settings and find the option to clear browsing data or cookies for this site.
