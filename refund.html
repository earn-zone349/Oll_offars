💳 Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Our policy on payments and refunds.

✅ EarnZone Pro is a FREE-TO-USE platform. Users do not pay to join or use the service. Earnings come from completing offers.

No Purchase Required

EarnZone Pro does not charge any registration or membership fees. Users earn money by completing tasks — there is no payment made by users to our platform. Therefore, traditional refund policies do not apply to account registration.

Withdrawal Reversals

Once a withdrawal request is submitted and approved:

  • Payments are non-reversible once processed.
  • If incorrect payment details are provided (wrong bKash number, wrong PayPal email, etc.), EarnZone Pro is not responsible for failed or misdirected payments.
  • Always double-check your payment details before submitting a withdrawal request.

Rejected Withdrawals

If a withdrawal request is rejected by our admin team:

  • The full balance is automatically returned to your account.
  • You will receive a notification in the app.
  • Common rejection reasons: insufficient balance, unmet referral requirements, suspected fraud.

Account Termination & Forfeiture

If your account is terminated due to violations of our Terms & Conditions (fraud, multiple accounts, bot usage), any pending earnings will be forfeited and are non-refundable. This is consistent with our anti-fraud policy.

Disputed Payments

If you believe a payment was sent to the wrong account or not received:

  • Contact us within 14 days of the expected payment date.
  • Provide your User ID, withdrawal amount, date, and payment method.
  • We will investigate and respond within 5 business days.
